Subtle environmental metaphors vs. overt symbolic devices.

Symbolism and visual motifs follow a similar pattern. Bernal relies on subtle environmental metaphors, such as sandstorms, barren hills, and long shadows, to represent spiritual emptiness and the blurred line between truth and illusion.
